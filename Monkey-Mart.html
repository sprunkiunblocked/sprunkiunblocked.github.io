Monkey Mart Overview

Game Concept

Monkey Mart is an idle/management game where you control a cute monkey character in charge of a supermarket. Plant fruits, harvest produce, and move around to fill the stands with various food items like bananas, corn, eggs, peanuts, coffee beans, chocolate, and wheat. Customers will pick up items and wait at the cashier desk for you to collect your money. As you unlock new aisles and grow your market, you can hire assistants and buy appliances to prepare advanced products like chocolate bars, coffee, yogurt, popcorn, and ice cream. How to Play Monkey Mart

Run around the market to grab fresh crops and stand next to the aisles to stack them up for customers. Your character will automatically do the work as long as you're in the right place!

  • Move: Use WASD or Arrow keys

Unlocking a New Mart

Pay attention to the spots the camera focuses on; you must save enough money to build an aisle there. Ensure you've unlocked all mandatory stalls to open your next branch.

Switching Between Marts

Tap the truck icon at the top of your screen to select and move between your branches. Alternatively, walk outside the shop and stand next to your truck.

Character Customization

Tap the hat icon at the top of your screen to browse the shop and check out the cool hats you can wear.

Earning More Money

Upgrading your mart is the best way to earn more money and unlock new things faster. Tap the icon with an arrow at the top of your screen to access the Upgrades menu, where you can improve:

  • Player: Helps carry more items at once for effective stacking.
  • Assistants, Farmers, Animals, and Chef: Increases their productivity, speed, and energy.
  • Appliances: Boosts the production capacity and durability of machines.
