Stickman Dragon Fight

Experience an amazing action game with powerful heroes fighting against evil!

Game Modes

  • Story Mode: Embark on an epic journey to explore the world and chase your enemies!
  • Versus Mode: Team up with 2 other heroes to fight against an opposing team.
  • Tournament Mode: Face off against 15 other teams to claim ultimate victory.

How to Play Stickman Dragon Fight

  • Move: A/D
  • Attack: Space bar
  • Block: B
  • Dash: F
  • Charge: Z
  • Special Ki: Q/W/R
  • Transform: S
