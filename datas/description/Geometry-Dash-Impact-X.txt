    <h2>About Geometry Dash Impact X</h2>
    <p><strong>Geometry Dash Impact X</strong> is a challenging rhythm-based platformer that has captivated gamers worldwide. Developed by RobTop Games, this installment introduces exciting new levels, obstacles, and features while retaining the core mechanics that fans of the series love.</p>

    <h3>Gameplay Guidelines</h3>
    <p>Playing <strong>Geometry Dash Impact X</strong> requires quick reflexes, precision, and a sense of rhythm. Here are some essential tips to help you master the game:</p>

    <h4>Basic Controls</h4>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Tap to Jump:</strong> Tap the screen or click the mouse to make your icon jump. Accurate timing is crucial for avoiding obstacles.</li>
        <li><strong>Hold to Fly:</strong> In certain levels, your icon may transform into a flying vehicle. Hold the screen or mouse to control its altitude and avoid obstacles.</li>
    </ul>

    <h4>Level Navigation</h4>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Practice Mode:</strong> Use practice mode to familiarize yourself with new levels. Place checkpoints to respawn from specific points instead of starting from the beginning.</li>
        <li><strong>Memorize Patterns:</strong> Pay attention to the patterns of obstacles and hazards. Repeated practice is key to improving your performance.</li>
        <li><strong>Timing is Everything:</strong> Synchronize your movements with the music’s rhythm to succeed in each level.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Advanced Tips</h3>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Custom Levels:</strong> Use the level editor to create and share your own levels. Explore user-generated content for new challenges.</li>
        <li><strong>Collect Orbs and Stars:</strong> Collecting orbs and stars in hard-to-reach areas unlocks new icons and customization options.</li>
        <li><strong>Use Boosts Wisely:</strong> Speed boosts and portals change your icon’s behavior. Learn to navigate these elements for smoother gameplay.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Customization and Community</h3>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Customize Your Icon:</strong> Unlock new shapes and colors by completing levels and collecting rewards. Personalize your icon for a unique gameplay experience.</li>
        <li><strong>Engage with the Community:</strong> Join the vibrant Geometry Dash Impact X community. Share your custom levels, learn strategies, and connect with fellow players.</li>
    </ul>
