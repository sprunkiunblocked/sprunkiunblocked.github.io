<section>
    <h2>What is Geometry Dash World?</h2>
    <p>Geometry Dash World is a super fun version of the original Geometry Dash game! It was released by RobTop Games on December 21, 2016, and it's part of the cool 2.1 update. This game is free to play, and it brings two awesome worlds to explore: <strong>Dashlands</strong> and <strong>Toxic Factory</strong>. Each world has 5 levels with fun and tricky challenges! The game is all about jumping to the beat of awesome music. Can you make it through all the levels without getting stuck?</p>
</section>

<section>
    <h2>How to Play?</h2>
    <ol>
        <li><strong>Start the Game:</strong> Go to GeoDash.org and click "Play" to begin your adventure in Geometry Dash World!</li>
        <li><strong>Basic Controls:</strong> Tap the screen, click the mouse, or press keyboard keys to make your cube character jump! Timing is important to avoid obstacles like spikes and gaps.</li>
        <li><strong>Advanced Moves:</strong> Tap or click repeatedly to jump multiple times in a row. In some parts, hold to glide through the air and release to fall down.</li>
        <li><strong>Goals and Tips:</strong> The goal is to complete each level without crashing! Keep practicing, and listen to the music — it will help you stay in rhythm and win!</li>
    </ol>
</section>

<section>
    <h2>Features of Geometry Dash World</h2>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Exclusive Music:</strong> Enjoy awesome tunes from artists like Dex Arson, Waterflame, and F-777. The music isn’t just fun to listen to — it helps you jump at the right time!</li>
        <li><strong>Key System:</strong> Find shiny keys while playing. These unlock new levels and secret areas. It’s like a treasure hunt in each level!</li>
        <li><strong>Gauntlets:</strong> Special level packs that tell a story. Finish a Gauntlet to earn cool rewards!</li>
        <li><strong>Short Levels:</strong> Each level is super quick, lasting only about 30 seconds! You can play in just a minute or two and still have a blast!</li>
        <li><strong>Diamond System:</strong> Collect diamonds by doing well in levels. You can use them to buy new stuff for your character!</li>
        <li><strong>Treasure Rooms:</strong> A special place to spend your diamonds on cool items like new icons and colors for your character.</li>
    </ul>
</section>

<section>
    <h2>How Many Levels in Geometry Dash World?</h2>
    <p>Geometry Dash World has 10 exciting levels, split into two cool worlds:</p>
    <h3>Dashland:</h3>
    <p>Dashland is the first world with five fun levels! It starts easy and gets more challenging. The levels are:</p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Payload</strong> (2 stars) - A simple introduction level.</li>
        <li><strong>Beast Mode</strong> (2 stars) - A little faster and harder!</li>
        <li><strong>Machina</strong> (3 stars) - More tricky timing and jumps.</li>
        <li><strong>Years</strong> (3 stars) - Harder obstacles and faster action!</li>
        <li><strong>Frontlines</strong> (3 stars) - The toughest level in Dashland!</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Toxic Factory:</h3>
    <p>Toxic Factory is the second world with five more levels. These are even more challenging, with cool music from different artists! The levels are:</p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Space Pirates</strong> (3 stars) - A space-themed level with gravity changes.</li>
        <li><strong>Striker</strong> (3 stars) - Fast and full of jumps and spins!</li>
        <li><strong>Embers</strong> (3 stars) - A fiery level with lots of flames.</li>
        <li><strong>Round 1</strong> (3 stars) - A boxing-themed level with strong beats!</li>
        <li><strong>Monster Dance Off</strong> (3 stars) - The final level with all your skills in action!</li>
    </ul>
</section>

<section>
    <h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>
    <h3>Is Geometry Dash World different from Geometry Dash?</h3>
    <p>Yes! Geometry Dash World is a smaller version with shorter levels and two cool worlds — Dashlands and Toxic Factory. It’s a free game, perfect for quick, fun plays!</p>

    <h3>Can I play Geometry Dash World for free?</h3>
    <p>Absolutely! You can play Geometry Dash World for free on iOS, Android, and PC. Just download it or play online!</p>

    <h3>What are some other Geometry Dash spin-offs?</h3>
    <p>Some other fun spin-offs of Geometry Dash include Geometry Dash Meltdown, Geometry Dash SubZero, and Geometry Dash Lite. Each one has its own unique levels and music!</p>
