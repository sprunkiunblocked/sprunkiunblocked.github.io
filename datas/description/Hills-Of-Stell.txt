<header>
    <h1>Hills of Steel</h1>
    <p>Get behind the wheel of a tank in this action-packed shooter game!</p>
</header>

<section>
    <h2>Game Overview</h2>
    <p>Hills of Steel puts you in control of a tank as you race and shoot your way through a hilled battlefield filled with enemies. Unlock a variety of tanks, mechs, and robots to use in your fight. Utilize powerful Boosters like mines, force shields, and rocket strikes to make your tank even more formidable. Can you build the ultimate tank and conquer the Hills of Steel?</p>
</section>

<section>
    <h2>How to Play Hills of Steel</h2>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Move:</strong> Use the A and D keys or the Arrow keys to drive your tank!</li>
        <li><strong>Shoot:</strong> Press the Spacebar to shoot!</li>
        <li><strong>Repair:</strong> Press the B button to repair your tank!</li>
    </ul>
</section>
