        <h2>Game Overview</h2>
        <p>From the creator of Ragdoll Archers comes <strong>Ragdoll Hit</strong>, where you control a ragdoll stickman to defeat various enemies and win battles. Use a combination of punches, kicks, jumps, and weapons to overcome your foes.</p>
        <p>Be aware of your surroundings and use them to gain the upper hand. The goal is to eliminate your enemies while minimizing your own injuries. Each victory earns you coins to purchase cool weapons and skins. Who will be the one and only champion of ragdoll fights?</p>
    </section>

    <section>
        <h2>How to Play Ragdoll Hit</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Hit and Move:</strong> A, D, or left and right arrow keys</li>
            <li><strong>Pick Up or Hold Items:</strong> Space bar</li>
            <li><strong>Jump:</strong> W or up arrow key</li>
            <li><strong>Kick:</strong> S or down arrow key</li>
        </ul>
