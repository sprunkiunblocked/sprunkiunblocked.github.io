<h2>Subway Surfers Overview</h2>

<h3>Game Concept</h3>
<p>Subway Surfers is a classic endless runner game where players take on the role of Jake, who is trying to escape from a grumpy Inspector and his dog. Players navigate through subway tracks, dodging trains and obstacles while collecting coins to unlock power-ups and characters.</p>

<h3>Platform</h3>
<p>Originally launched in 2012 by Sybo, Subway Surfers is now available as an HTML5 game, allowing play on mobile devices, tablets, and PCs through web browsers, especially on sites like Poki. It's free to play without downloads.</p>

<h3>Gameplay Mechanics</h3>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Left/Right Arrow:</strong> Move left/right</li>
    <li><strong>Up Arrow:</strong> Jump</li>
    <li><strong>Down Arrow:</strong> Roll</li>
    <li><strong>Space:</strong> Activate hoverboard</li>
</ul>
