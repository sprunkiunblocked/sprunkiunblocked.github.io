            <li><strong>Casual Survival Mode:</strong> Turn PvP on or off for a more relaxed experience.</li>

        </ul>
        <p>With so many options available, where will you start your adventure in Vectaria.io?</p>

    <section>
        <h2>How to Play Vectaria.io</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Movement:</strong> WASD or arrow keys</li>
            <li><strong>Place Block:</strong> Right mouse button</li>
            <li><strong>Mine / Attack:</strong> Left mouse button</li>
            <li><strong>Jump:</strong> Spacebar</li>
            <li><strong>Open Inventory:</strong> X</li>
            <li><strong>Open Home Menu:</strong> G</li>
            <li><strong>View Spawn Points:</strong> M</li>
            <li><strong>Open Shop:</strong> O</li>
            <li><strong>Open Guide:</strong> I</li>
            <li><strong>Chat:</strong> Press Enter then type!</li>
        </ul>
        <p>To change PvP mode on or off, go into the inventory (press <code>X</code>) and look in the top left corner to toggle it!</p>
