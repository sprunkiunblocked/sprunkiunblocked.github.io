DMCA Policy

How to Submit a DMCA Notice

  1. Identify the copyrighted work you believe has been infringed.
  2. Provide the exact URL(s) where the infringing material is located on our site.
  3. Include your contact information (name, address, phone, email).
  4. Include a statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  5. Include a statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf.
  6. Sign the notice (electronic signature is acceptable).

Send your DMCA notice to: sprunkiwebgames@gmail.com

What Happens Next?

  • If the claim is valid, we will remove or disable access to the infringing material.

Counter-Notification

Please include your contact information, the material removed, and a statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the removal was a mistake. Send counter-notifications to the same email above.
